Pat's Story

Pat's love for salmon and cookies started years ago He and his family would go fishing during salmon season. His mother would always pack a few cookies to have a little treat thoughtout the day. One day it hit him; why not combine his two favorite thing? Since starting salmon cookies in his home kitchen in 2019. More than 50 trillion cookies have been sold in various cities throughou the U.S.
